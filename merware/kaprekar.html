Overview

KaprekarApp puts Kaprekar’s algorithm at your fingertips with a cross‑platform experience for Windows and Android — available free on the Microsoft Store and Google Play Store.
Built with Microsoft’s .NET and MAUI frameworks, it delivers seamless performance across mobile and desktop.
For all my nerds out there, the app uses a unique “Wow! Signal” epoch‑based versioning system — a playful nod to extraterrestrial time (ET).
And to top it off, it features a debut background rap track celebrating the algorithm and Kaprekar’s biography.

Privacy Policy: KaprekarApp by MerWare does not collect, store, or share any personal data, usage data, or analytics. No telemetry or tracking is performed; the app runs entirely locally on your device. No information is transmitted to MerWare or third parties. For questions, contact support@merware.com.
Security Commitment: While on install/update it warns "This app can: Uses all system resources" this is a permissions artifact required by the MAUI framework which, like KaprekarApp, is in its early stages of maturity. MerWare is committed to ensuring the app operates at the lowest privilege level allowed by the framework. Future updates will adjust permissions as the MAUI framework evolves to minimize exposure.
Transparency: Users can be assured that no hidden data collection, profiling, or third-party sharing occurs. The app is designed for educational and entertainment purposes only, with privacy and security as core principles.
